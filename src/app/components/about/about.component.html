About Us

Since 2018 , I aspired to be an entrepreneur but struggled to begin due to my middle-class background. My father’s retail work and mother’s homemaking shaped my perspective, yet uncertainty impacted my health, worsening PCOD. Heeding my mother’s advice, I prioritized wellness—through coaching, nutrition, and community support—I reversed my condition, losing 10 kg in three months. During the pandemic, I joined a wellness community, guiding others battling health issues, which revealed the transformative power of lifestyle changes. This experience, coupled with interactions with homemakers, showed me the potential of women leading change.

This inspired “Live Your Best Life,” a platform to empower individuals, especially homemakers and youth, with trusted wellness support. Our mission is rooted in personal triumph and proven community impact—ensuring you’ll never regret joining this journey.
